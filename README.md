Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26 December 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Basket Hoop (“the App”) handles user data.

1. Information Collection and Use

The App does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information such as name, email address, phone number, or location.
The App is designed as a casual offline game and can be used without providing any personal data.

2. Automatically Collected Information

The App may use standard platform services (Android) that can automatically collect non-personal technical data, such as:
device type
operating system version
app version
anonymous crash or performance data
This information is used only to improve stability and performance of the App.

3. Third-Party Services

At the current alpha stage:
The App does not use analytics services
The App does not display ads
The App does not include in-app purchases
If third-party services (such as ads or analytics) are added in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

4. Data Security

Since the App does not collect personal data, no personal information is stored or transmitted.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13.
As no personal data is collected at all, the App is considered safe for general audiences.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future.
Any changes will be published with a revised “Last updated” date.
